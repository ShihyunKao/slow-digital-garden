# Open V04: Held Imprint

## Question
Can a sustained open-palm gesture make a hand-based trace more reliable and intentional without adding unnecessary steps?

## Change from V03
V03 triggered an imprint as soon as a fixed hand-open threshold was crossed. V04 keeps the scale-independent hand measurement but removes the manual calibration step because it did not create a meaningful experience for the visitor. Instead, the palm must remain open for about 0.3 seconds before an imprint is created.

## Interaction

1. Press `M` to enable the camera.
2. Relax the hand, then open the palm slowly.
3. Hold the palm until the small ring around the wrist closes.
4. Relax again before making the next imprint.

## Keys

- `M`: camera / mouse input
- `P`: hand display mode
- `R`: clear imprints
- `?`: help screen

## Testing focus

- Does the visual progress ring make the required short pause understandable?
- Does the gesture feel deliberate rather than delayed?
- Does it still fail when the palm is strongly side-on or leaves the camera frame?
