# Open V03: Stellar Unfurl

## Question
Can the opening of one hand unfold a small cosmic field and leave a memory of the gesture?

## Change from V02
V02 explored layered blooming forms. V03 shifts the visual language toward the cosmic direction that emerged in Both Hands V02 and V03. The hand no longer opens a flower-like object; it opens a seed-like constellation field.

## Interaction
In Mouse Mode, horizontal mouse movement controls the opening amount.

Pressing M activates HandPose Mode. The openness of one hand controls the field. Opening the hand expands the constellation. Closing the hand after a full opening leaves a short-lived memory trace.

Pressing R clears the memory traces.

## Design intention
This sketch connects the single-hand opening movement to the larger cosmic memory system. The gesture becomes a small act of unfolding, rather than a direct drawing action.

## Technical experiment
- One-hand openness detection
- Mouse fallback mode
- Smooth interpolation
- Gesture threshold detection
- Radial orbit forms
- Temporary constellation memory

## What to observe
- Does one-hand opening feel clear and intentional?
- Is the cosmic visual language stronger than the earlier flower direction?
- Does the memory trace make the gesture feel more meaningful?
- Could this become a smaller exercise within the final interaction system?

## Next development
Test whether the open gesture can become a preparatory movement before the larger two-hand breathing interaction.
