# 01 Opening Flower

## Question
Can an open-palm gesture create a gentle, understandable flower-opening interaction?

## Interaction
The openness of one hand controls the size of a digital flower.

## What worked
- The relationship between an open hand and an opening flower is intuitive.
- The interaction is calm and does not require speed.

## To test next
- Does slow movement feel different from fast movement?
- Should a flower remain after the hand closes?
- Would softer colours and sound improve the experience?
